# Hospitality Flow

## Motto

**AI that helps boutique hotels run better.**

## Vision

To become the leading AI operations platform for boutique hotels, helping teams simplify daily operations, preserve hotel knowledge, and deliver exceptional guest experiences.

## Why We Exist

Hotels should not lose operational knowledge whenever an experienced employee leaves.

Teams should not waste time searching emails, WhatsApp messages, spreadsheets, noticeboards, and paper notes just to understand what is happening.

Hospitality Flow creates a central Hotel Knowledge Layer that powers practical AI tools built specifically for boutique hotel operations.

We do not replace hospitality teams. We help them work faster, make better decisions, improve consistency, and spend more time supporting guests.

## Core Platform

- Hotel Profile and Knowledge Layer
- AI Shift Handover
- AI Operations Generator
- AI SOP Generator
- AI Rota Generator
- Future AI modules

## Principles

- Solve real hotel problems.
- Keep every tool simple and practical.
- Build for boutique hotels and smaller hotel groups first.
- AI should reduce workload, not create more work.
- Every feature must provide clear operational value.
- Hotel knowledge should belong to the hotel, not remain only in employees’ memories.
- Build one adaptable platform rather than separate versions for every hotel.

## Long-Term Goal

Create the AI operations platform that boutique hotels rely on every day.

> Build something hotel teams genuinely enjoy using.
